# QuoteForge Decisions

This document records important product decisions.

The purpose is to avoid revisiting decisions without good reason.

---

## Decision 001

QuoteForge is a business management platform for self-employed tradespeople.

It is NOT an AI application.

AI exists only to assist the user.

---

## Decision 002

Version 1 focuses on helping sole traders and businesses with 1–10 employees.

We will not try to build for large companies initially.

---

## Decision 003

Every business has its own secure workspace.

Users only ever see their own customers, proposals and business information.

---

## Decision 004

Every proposal receives its own unique proposal number.

Example:

QF-2026-0001

---

## Decision 005

Every customer belongs to one workspace.

Over time a customer will build a history of:

- proposals
- jobs
- notes
- invoices
- payments

---

## Decision 006

Version 1 must stay small.

If a feature does not help us obtain the first ten paying customers it belongs inside IDEAS.md.

---

## Decision 007

Every development session should finish with QuoteForge in a working state.

No half-finished features.

---

## Decision 008

AI should never make decisions for the user.

AI should only assist.

The tradesperson always has the final decision.

---

## Decision 009

Every feature must satisfy at least one of these:

• Saves time

• Reduces paperwork

• Helps the user look more professional

• Helps the user win more work

---

## Decision 010

We are building a business.

Not just software.

The dashboard should reduce stress, not create it.
