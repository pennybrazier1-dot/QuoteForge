# Our Mission

We're building software for people who spend their day doing the work, not sitting behind a desk.

Our customers are plumbers, builders, electricians, landscapers, carpenters, roofers, decorators, kitchen fitters, fencing contractors and hundreds of other independent tradespeople.

Most of them don't have office staff.

Most of them don't have a PA.

Most of them spend their evenings catching up on paperwork.

We believe that should change.

Our mission is simple:

Give hardworking tradespeople their evenings back.

Every feature we build should remove paperwork, reduce stress and save time.

If a feature makes the product more complicated, it doesn't belong.

Simple beats clever.

Useful beats impressive.

Helping people beats adding features.
